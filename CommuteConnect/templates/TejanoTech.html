About Me

Tejano Technology is an Austin based tech aggregator, with the community in mind. Our mission is to bring technology in to our at risk neighborhoods and schools, supplying youth who would not normally have an oppurtinity to succeed with the advantages others might be supplied with. Tejano Technology believes the most important step moving forward is diversifying the tech industry, and including new perspectives to bring to the table. The Austin Chronicle has voted us a "top 10 place to work" and our company has grown at an accelerated rate since we began 4 years ago.
